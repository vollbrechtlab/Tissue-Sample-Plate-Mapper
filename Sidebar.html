How to use this add-on

  1. Put your data in "Original Data" sheet.
    Be sure "Plot Name/#", "Plant #" and "Note (optional)" are spelled correctly
  2. Fill out the form in this sidebar.
  3. Go to "Plate Labels for printing" sheet.
  4. Print the sheet with the following settings.
    Print: Current sheet,
    Page orientation: Landscape,
    Scale: 100%,
    Margins: Normal,
    Formatting: No gridlines,
    Horizontal Alignment: Center,
    Vertical Alignment: Center
    (Some browsers save PDF instead of printing)
